# NomadAI - Business Overview

> Executive summary for stakeholders, investors, and business teams

---

## What is NomadAI?

**NomadAI** is a voice-first AI assistant for hotels that:
1. **Reduces front desk workload** by 70-90%
2. **Increases guest satisfaction** with <2 second response times
3. **Drives revenue** through personalized upselling (+23% ancillary spend)

---

## The Problem We Solve

| Problem | Impact | Our Solution |
|---------|--------|--------------|
| Staff shortages | 30-35% labor costs | AI handles routine queries |
| Slow response times | 12+ min average | <2 second voice responses |
| Language barriers | Lost international guests | 20+ language support |
| Missed upsells | Lost revenue | AI-driven recommendations |
| OTA commissions | 15-25% per booking | Direct booking incentives |

## Target Market

### B2B: Hotels
- Independent & boutique hotels

- **TAM**: 1.4 billion international arrivals/year
